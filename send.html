EHCP Support

Understanding Education, Health and Care Plans

Education, Health and Care Plans (EHCPs) can often feel overwhelming for families navigating the SEND system.

We provide practical support with requests for EHC needs assessments, reviewing professional evidence, preparing representations and helping families understand the process from start to finish.

Every child is different and ensuring their needs are clearly identified and properly evidenced can make a significant difference to the support they receive.

Areas We Can Assist With

  • EHC Needs Assessment Requests
  • Reviewing Draft EHCPs
  • Preparing Supporting Evidence
  • Annual Reviews
  • School Placement Issues
Annual Reviews & Placements

Supporting Families Through Important Decisions

Annual reviews and school placement decisions can have a significant impact upon a child's education and wellbeing.

We can assist families in reviewing proposed amendments, considering educational provision and preparing evidence relating to suitable school placements.

Every child's circumstances are unique and ensuring that educational provision properly reflects their needs is essential.

Support May Include

  • Reviewing Annual Review Documentation
  • School Placement Considerations
  • Preparing Written Representations
  • Reviewing Professional Evidence
  • Tribunal Preparation Support
